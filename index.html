VizCareer’s technology product is designed to dramatically improve the way in which clients can identify and engage with specific talent pools, subsequently allowing potential candidates to engage back into a recruitment process, all on one integrated platform. Testimonial

VizCareer recently completed a 60 hire campaign for ExxonMobil in record time, with record number of hires (the most they had achieved before was 20 in one campaign)and at a dramatically reduced cost.

This campaign has been tremendously successful, for several reasons. The main ones being the quality and diversity of response to the video adverts coupled with the video review sessions; these in particular allowed us to “introduce” the candidates to the managers/supervisors who otherwise would not have met them until the final interviews/assessment. This really softened the impact of subjectivity for us as a team. We really enjoyed doing business with VizCareer; they are ethical, driven, introduce innovation and are totally committed to delivering to the customer needs.
